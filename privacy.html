Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

Formulate ("we", "our", "us") is a desktop application for tracking and optimizing your supplement stack. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Desktop Application

Formulate runs entirely on your local machine. Your health data, supplement stacks, scores, and preferences are stored in a local SQLite database on your computer. We do not have access to this data.

  • No cloud sync. Your data stays on your device.
  • No telemetry. The app does not phone home or send usage analytics.
  • No account required. You can use the full app without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Website & Waitlist

When you visit formulate-health.app or join the waitlist, we may collect:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily submit it via the waitlist form. We use Formspree to process submissions.
  • Anonymous analytics — we use Vercel Web Analytics, which collects aggregate page view data without cookies or personal identifiers.

How We Use Your Data

  • Waitlist emails are used solely to notify you about Formulate updates, early access, and launch announcements. We will never sell your email or share it with third parties.
  • Analytics data helps us understand how many people visit our site and which pages are most useful. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — hosting and anonymous web analytics
  • Formspree — waitlist form processing
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We do not share any personal data with other third parties.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies. Vercel Web Analytics is cookie-free and does not track individual users.

Data Retention

  • Desktop app data is stored locally and persists until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all data.
  • Waitlist emails are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (limited to your waitlist email, if provided)
  • Delete your data by contacting us
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time

Children's Privacy

Formulate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Formulate after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at hello@formulate-health.app.
