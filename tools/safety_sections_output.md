<p>

</p>

<Callout variant="warning" title="If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or trying to conceive">
  Berberine crosses the placenta and has demonstrated teratogenic effects in animal models. It is contraindicated during pregnancy. </Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="If you are 65 or older">
  Older adults face higher rates of polypharmacy, reduced renal function, and increased susceptibility to hypoglycemia — all of which amplify berberine's interaction and safety risks. </Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="If you take diabetes medications (metformin, sulfonylureas, or insulin)">
  Combining berberine with existing glucose-lowering drugs increases hypoglycemia risk, particularly with sulfonylureas or insulin. Your physician may need to adjust doses or increase glucose monitoring frequency. Do not add berberine to a diabetes regimen without clinical oversight.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="If you have kidney or liver impairment">
