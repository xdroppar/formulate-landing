Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 11, 2026

The short version: Formulate may earn a small commission when you purchase supplements through links in our app. This never influences our scores — every product is rated by the same algorithm regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship with the brand.

How We Earn Revenue

Formulate is free to use. To keep the lights on, we participate in affiliate programs with select supplement retailers and brands. When you click a link in Formulate to purchase a product and complete a purchase, we may receive a small referral commission at no additional cost to you.

We may participate in affiliate programs including, but not limited to:

  • Amazon Associates
  • iHerb Affiliate Program
  • Direct brand affiliate programs (e.g., Thorne, Nootropics Depot)
  • Affiliate networks (e.g., ShareASale, Impact, CJ Affiliate)

What This Means for You

  • You pay the same price. Affiliate commissions come from the retailer's marketing budget, not from a markup on your purchase.
  • Scores are never influenced. Our scoring algorithm is deterministic and runs identically for every product. Having or not having an affiliate relationship with a brand has zero impact on their score.
  • We link to the best option. If a product is available from multiple retailers, we aim to link to the most convenient and reputable source — not necessarily the one with the highest commission.

Scoring Independence

Our commitment to independent scoring is fundamental to Formulate's value. Here's how we ensure it:

  • Algorithmic scoring — Products are scored by a deterministic algorithm. The same inputs always produce the same score. There are no manual overrides.
  • No pay-to-play — We do not accept payments from supplement manufacturers in exchange for higher scores, better placement, or favorable reviews.
  • No sponsored content — Product pages in Formulate are generated from our scoring engine, not from brand-provided marketing copy.
  • Full methodology disclosure — Our complete scoring methodology is published publicly so you can understand exactly how every score is calculated.

How to Identify Affiliate Links

Links to external retailers within the Formulate app (such as "Buy" or "View on" buttons on product detail pages) may be affiliate links. These links take you to the retailer's website where you can make your own purchase decision.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). We believe in full transparency about our business relationships.
